Project Overview

Studying the impact of soft infrastructure changes on pedestrian safety across Minneapolis. We leverage existing CCTV feeds and archival footage to quantify how design interventions influence real-world street behavior.

Our AI-based assessment framework ingests municipal CCTV streams, performs privacy-preserving detections, and outputs metrics that city partners can act on. The modular pipeline makes it easy to adopt, whether cities maintain expansive camera networks or only a handful of intersections.

Capabilities

  • Rapid deployment using existing CCTV or archived footage
  • Scalable architecture for neighborhood to city-wide analysis
  • Computer-vision driven metrics for planners and policy teams

Analytics Toolkit

  • Speed analysis for traffic calming evaluations
  • Behavioral insights on stop compliance and motion direction
  • Temporal trend dashboards for before/after studies
