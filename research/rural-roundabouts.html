Project Overview

We evaluate emerging rural roundabout (RAB) designs to reduce severe crashes on high-speed corridors. Edge computer-vision pipelines estimate vehicle speeds, occupancy, and approach patterns to inform design refinements.

Insights generated with SRF Consulting and MnDOT guide context-sensitive solutions for communities transitioning from traditional intersections to modern roundabouts.

Focus Areas

  • New roundabout concept evaluation for rural settings
  • Speed behavior modeling for entering, circulating, and exit lanes
  • AI-based speed estimation from roadside sensors
  • Occupancy detection for dynamic flow adjustments

Deployment Insights

  • Configurable dashboards for SRF design workshops
  • Automation-ready data exports for MnDOT review
  • Edge pipelines resilient to rural connectivity gaps
